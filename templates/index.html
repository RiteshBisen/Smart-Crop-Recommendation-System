This system analyzes your soil parameters and environmental conditions to recommend the most suitable crops using agricultural science principles.

Parameter Guide:

  • 🍃 Nitrogen (N): Essential for plant growth and leaf development
  • 🔥 Phosphorus (P): Important for root development and flowering
  • ⚡ Potassium (K): Helps with disease resistance and fruit quality
  • 🌡️ Temperature: Average temperature during growing season
  • 💧 Humidity: Relative humidity in the air
  • ⚗️ pH Level: Soil acidity/alkalinity level
  • 🌧️ Rainfall: Annual precipitation amount
